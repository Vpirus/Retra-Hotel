Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury

Founded in 1992, Retra Hotel began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to create an urban sanctuary that redefines luxury hospitality. What started as a boutique establishment with just 20 rooms has evolved into one of Melbourne's most prestigious hotels, renowned for its impeccable service and timeless elegance.

Our name "Retra" comes from the Latin word "retrosum," meaning to look back or reflect. We believe in honoring traditional hospitality values while embracing modern innovations to create unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Over three decades, we have welcomed dignitaries, celebrities, and travelers from around the world, each contributing to our rich tapestry of stories. Through careful renovations and expansions, we've preserved our historical charm while integrating contemporary amenities that today's discerning travelers expect.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Retra Hotel, we believe luxury and sustainability go hand in hand. Our comprehensive approach to environmental stewardship touches every aspect of our operations.

Water Conservation

Our advanced water recycling systems and low-flow fixtures have reduced our water consumption by 40% since 2018.

Renewable Energy

Solar panels on our roof provide 30% of our energy needs, with plans to increase to 50% by 2027.

Farm-to-Table Dining

Our kitchen sources 80% of ingredients from local farmers and producers within a 100km radius.

Waste Reduction

Our comprehensive recycling program diverts 85% of our waste from landfills, including our innovative food waste composting.

Proud recipient of EarthCheck Gold Certification for sustainable tourism operations since 2019.
