Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in select room packages. Please check your reservation details or contact our reservations team to confirm if breakfast is included in your stay.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services for an additional fee. Please contact our concierge at least 24 hours in advance to arrange transportation.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Our standard cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival may be subject to a one-night charge. Special rates and promotions may have different cancellation policies.
